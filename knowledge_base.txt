Company Leave Policy
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. PTO is accrued monthly.

Unused PTO can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days. Any excess will be forfeited.

Sick leave is 12 days per year and requires a doctor's note for absences longer than 2 consecutive days. Sick leave is separate from PTO.

Parental leave is 16 weeks for the primary caregiver and 4 weeks for the secondary caregiver, fully paid.

Bereavement leave is 5 days for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling).

Work From Home (WFH) & Hybrid Work Policy
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week, with manager approval. Department-specific schedules may apply.

Core working hours are from 10 AM to 4 PM local time. Employees must be available for communication during these hours.

The company will provide a one-time stipend of P25,000 for home office setup for all new full-time employees.

Employees are expected to maintain a professional and secure home work environment. Company IT equipment must be used for work purposes only and protected from damage or theft.

Code of Conduct & Professional Ethics
All employees are expected to maintain a high level of professionalism, integrity, and respect in all interactions with colleagues, clients, and partners.

Harassment and discrimination of any kind are strictly prohibited. This includes behavior based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, national origin, or disability.

Conflicts of interest, whether real or perceived, must be disclosed to HR immediately.

Company resources, including internet, email, and equipment, are for business use. Personal use should be minimal and must not interfere with work responsibilities.

Health and Wellness Benefits
All regular employees are enrolled in the company's comprehensive health insurance plan (HMO) from their first day of employment, which covers hospitalization, outpatient care, and dental services.

The company provides free annual physical exams (APE) for all employees.

A monthly wellness allowance of P2,000 can be used for gym memberships, fitness classes, or mental health services.

The Employee Assistance Program (EAP) offers confidential counseling and support for personal and work-related challenges.

Performance Management and Review Cycle
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually, in June and December.

The review process includes self-assessment, manager evaluation, and setting goals (OKRs) for the next cycle.

Promotions and salary adjustments are primarily based on the year-end performance review results and overall company performance.

Continuous feedback is encouraged. Employees should have regular check-ins with their managers outside of the formal review cycle.

Travel and Expense Policy
All work-related travel must be pre-approved by a department head.

Employees will be reimbursed for reasonable expenses incurred during business travel, including airfare, accommodation, and meals.

Expense reports must be submitted through the company portal with all corresponding receipts within 15 days of trip completion.

For local transportation, the company reimburses ride-sharing services for client meetings. Daily commute to the office is not covered.

IT, Equipment, and Security Policy
The IT department will issue a company laptop and other necessary peripherals to all employees.

Employees are responsible for the care and security of their issued equipment. Lost or damaged equipment must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

Using strong, unique passwords and enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all company accounts.

Do not install unauthorized software on company laptops. All software must be approved and installed by the IT department.
